Who We Are

Shipping Sucks. We Fix It.

SABIT was built to solve a frustrating truth: Most suppliers, freight forwarders or shippers overpromise, underdeliver, and vanish when things go wrong. We do it differently. SABIT is your trust logistics advisor – a boutique, high-touch freight support platform that equips you with confidence, full visibility, and control at every step of your supply chain. We don’t just “book shipments.”We work with a curated network of vetted agents worldwide to ensure nothing falls through the cracks – from pickup to final delivery.
